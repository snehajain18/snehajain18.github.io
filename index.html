About Me

The analyst behind the numbers

I'm a Business Analyst with deep expertise in healthcare and CPG marketing analytics — specializing in Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Tableau, and end-to-end data pipeline design. Over the last two years I've been embedded in enterprise-scale customer journey programs, working at the intersection of data engineering, campaign strategy, and executive reporting.
